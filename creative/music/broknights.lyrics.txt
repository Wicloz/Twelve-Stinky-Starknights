[Intro]
Twelve shadows on the horizon, twelve boots in the dust,
Steel in their hands and a mission they trust.
The factory lights are burning bright,
The whole damn planet knows:
The Starnights build tonight.

[Verse 1]
Twelve big hearts and a will of stone,
Building an empire they call their own.
Sweat on their brows, sparks in the sky,
They move like thunder when the machines come alive.

They carry the load, they stand in the flame,
Every single knight knows every name.
No one gets left, no one falls behind,
Twelve brothers of the grind.

[Chorus]
Twelve Starnights, marching in line,
Shoulder to shoulder, every time.
Strong as steel, bright as chrome,
They turned this wasteland into home.

Twelve Starnights, hear the turbines roar,
Building bigger than ever before.
Raise up your hands, let the whole world know:
The Starnight spirit never lets go.

[Verse 2]
One brings the steel, one brings the fire,
One keeps pushing the factory higher.
One counts the numbers, one guards the gate,
One says "we're early", when everyone’s late.

They laugh at the danger, they stare down the night,
A ridiculous army of unstoppable might.
Covered in grease, covered in pride,
With twelve best friends standing side by side.

[Bridge]
When the planets are cold, when the stars disappear,
They’ll still be building year after year.
Through every storm and every fight,
They’ll remember this:
Twelve stood together that night.

[Final Chorus]
Twelve Starnights, marching in line,
Legends are forged in the furnace shine.
Not made of gold, not made of fame,
They built tomorrow in their name.

Twelve Starnights, forever they stand,
The strongest crew across the land.
The machines may stop, the skies may fall,
But the Starnight bond outlasts it all.

[Outro]
Twelve lights burning in the dark,
Twelve hands leaving their mark.
No throne, no crown, no victory prize—
Just twelve Starnights beneath alien skies.
